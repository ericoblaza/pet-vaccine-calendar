HOW TO CHANGE XAMPP PORT TO 8080:

1. Open XAMPP Control Panel
2. Click "Config" button next to Apache
3. Select "httpd.conf"
4. Find these lines (around line 60 and 180):
   - Listen 80
   - ServerName localhost:80
5. Change them to:
   - Listen 8080
   - ServerName localhost:8080
6. Save the file
7. In XAMPP Control Panel, click "Config" → "Service and Port Settings"
8. Change Apache Main Port to 8080
9. Click "Save"
10. Start Apache

Then access your app at: http://localhost:8080/vaccine_calendar/
