OPTION 1: USE DIRECTLY (EASIEST - NO SERVER NEEDED)
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────
1. Double-click: OPEN_APP.bat
2. App opens in your browser
3. Everything works! (Calendar, appointments, reminders, etc.)

This works perfectly for local use on your computer!


OPTION 2: FOR ANDROID PHONE ACCESS
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Method A: Simple Python Server
1. Double-click: start-server.bat
2. Find your computer's IP address:
   - Open Command Prompt
   - Type: ipconfig
   - Look for "IPv4 Address" (e.g., 192.168.1.100)
3. On Android phone (same WiFi):
   - Open Chrome
   - Go to: http://YOUR_IP:8000
   - Example: http://192.168.1.100:8000

Method B: Fix XAMPP Port
1. Follow instructions in: FIX_XAMPP_PORT.txt
2. Change Apache port to 8080
3. Access at: http://localhost:8080/vaccine_calendar/


OPTION 3: TRANSFER TO PHONE
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────
1. Copy entire vaccine_calendar folder to your phone
2. Open index.html in Chrome on your phone
3. Works offline!
