═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
STEP-BY-STEP:
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. Go to: https://github.com
   - Sign up (free) or log in

2. Click "+" → "New repository"
   - Name: pet-vaccine-calendar
   - Make it PUBLIC (required!)
   - Check "Add README"
   - Click "Create repository"

3. Click "Add file" → "Upload files"
   - Drag ALL files from vaccine_calendar folder:
     ✓ index.html
     ✓ app.js
     ✓ styles.css
     ✓ manifest.json
     ✓ sw.js
     ✓ README.md
     ✓ create-icons.html (optional)
   - Click "Commit changes"

4. Go to "Settings" → "Pages"
   - Source: "Deploy from a branch"
   - Branch: main
   - Folder: / (root)
   - Click "Save"

5. Wait 2 minutes, then your app is live at:
   https://YOUR_USERNAME.github.io/pet-vaccine-calendar/
